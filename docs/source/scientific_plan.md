The plan is to reproduce the ["Electrochemical solid phase synthesis"](https://patents.google.com/patent/US6280595B1/en) patent. A more modern webinar version of this technology is available [from Genscript](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gAH2kwvc7vM).

## DNA synthesis basics

DNA synthesis, or "oligonucleotide synthesis" is a process of building a desired DNA sequence. It adds base pairs one at a time to a growing DNA chain through a process of de-blocking, coupling, capping, and oxidation. The general idea is to continuously add to a chain, capping off the ~0.1%-1% of sequences that fail, until you get a chain of the desired length.
